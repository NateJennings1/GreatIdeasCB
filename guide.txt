The ena website contains "shotgun metagenomic data" for a few hundred individuals.

The filereport json contains a lot of information, but one of the most useful is the 
correspondance between samples and individual's identifer. 
The supplementary data of the paper contains phenotypic and subject metadata (diagnosis)
as well as their identifier.

The raw data in raw data is just the sample however, so it must be tied to the individual 
by looking at the json file.
